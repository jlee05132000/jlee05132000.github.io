Camping
Menu

Three days of fire-cooked meals, marinated meats, and one upgraded ramen for the road home. Prep what you can at home — counter space at the cabin is precious.

Campfire 🔥 Stovetop ☕ Featured · Date Night

Saturday ☀️

Day 01 · Settle in & cook over the fire
Dinner
Campfire 🔥
Korean BBQ 🥩
Bulgogi / Galbi ♡
Ingredients
Pre-marinated meat (H Mart!), lettuce wraps, perilla leaves, gochujang, sesame oil, instant rice cups
Marinate meat before leaving home. H Mart in Doraville has amazing pre-marinated options ♡
+ Side
Campfire 🔥
Corn on the Cob 🌽
Ingredients
Corn, butter, tajín or salt
Soak in water 30 min. Grill in husk, peel & eat with butter + tajín ✦
Dessert
Campfire 🔥
S'mores 🍫
Ingredients
Graham crackers, chocolate, marshmallows
Classic. No notes needed 🥹

Sunday 🌿

Day 02 · Long day, three meals on the fire
Breakfast
Stovetop ☕
Miso Soup
Ingredients
Miso paste, silken tofu, green onions
Start miso first — just hot water + paste.
Lunch
Campfire 🔥
Hot Dogs 🌭
+ Sweet Potato Foil Packets
Ingredients
Hot dogs, buns, condiments; sweet potatoes, bell peppers, zucchini, onion, butter, Italian seasoning
Pre-chop veggies at home in zip bags. Foil packets go in coals 25 min ahead ♡
Dinner
Campfire 🔥
Steak Night 🥩
+ Potato Foil Packets & Corn
Ingredients
Ribeye or NY strip, salt, pepper, garlic butter; russet potatoes, rosemary; corn
Season steak at home. Let it rest to room temp before grilling. This is date night ✦
Dessert
Campfire 🔥
Grilled Pineapple 🍍
Ingredients
Fresh pineapple rings, brown sugar, cinnamon
Place cut-side down on grate until caramelized ~5 min. Magical 🥹

Monday 🌅

Day 03 · Last morning before the drive home
Breakfast
Stovetop ☕
Upgraded Ramen 🍜
Ingredients
Instant ramen, eggs, sriracha OR coconut milk + chili crisp, green onions
Quick & warming before the drive home. Use leftover green onions from miso night ✿

Prep at home

★ Marinate & Season

  • Bulgogi / galbi from H Mart (or marinate ribeye in soy-sesame-garlic 24 hrs ahead)
  • Steak: salt, pepper, garlic powder rub the night before
  • Sweet potatoes & russets — scrub, leave whole

★ Pre-Chop & Bag

  • Bell peppers, zucchini, onions for foil packets — zip bags
  • Green onions chopped (for both miso & ramen)
  • Pineapple rings for dessert — pre-sliced container

★ Pantry & Dry

  • Instant rice cups · instant ramen packets
  • Miso paste · gochujang · sesame oil · chili crisp
  • Brown sugar + cinnamon shaker · tajín · Italian seasoning
  • Graham crackers · chocolate · marshmallows

★ Cook Gear

  • Heavy-duty foil (lots of it)
  • Cast iron grate or pan for the steak sear
  • Small camp stove + fuel for miso & ramen
  • Tongs · skewers · oven mitt · trash bags
