Field notes

★ Cell Service

Drops out about 15 minutes from the cabin and stays spotty around Suches. Download offline maps before you turn off GA-60. Expect no coverage on the Cooper Creek and Swinging Bridge trails.

★ Groceries & Provisions

No real grocery store near the cabin. Stock up in Dahlonega on Saturday before heading up. Walmart in Blue Ridge is the backup if you forget anything — about 35 min from the cabin.

★ FS Road 816 Closure

The direct dirt road to the Swinging Bridge parking lot is currently closed. Access is via the Benton MacKaye Trail from GA Hwy 60 — a 3.5-mile hike each way. Confirm conditions with the Blue Ridge Ranger District before Monday.

★ Wolf Mountain Reservations

Saturday tables fill up. Book ahead. Wine tasting closes at 5pm. Tasting fee is around $50/person — bottle & brunch is the better play if you want lunch.

★ Brasstown Bald Logistics

$8/person entrance and shuttle fee (free for kids under 16). Shuttle goes from the parking area to the summit, or hike the steep 0.6-mile trail. Off-season bathrooms are porta-johns only.

★ Driving the Mountain

The road into Suches gets steep and windy at the end, with sharp switchbacks. Arrive before dark for easier navigation. If anyone in the car is prone to motion sickness, plan accordingly.
